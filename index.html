OUR MISSION

The Women in Pre-Law Society is dedicated to creating a welcoming, supportive, and empowering community for undergraduate women at Northeastern University who are interested in exploring the field of law. Our inclusive environment ensures that there are no barriers to entry, allowing all members to connect with like-minded individuals, access valuable resources, and grow personally and professionally. Through networking, academic support, and career development opportunities, we aim to inspire and prepare our members for their legal aspirations.

HOW TO JOIN

Women in Pre-Law is open to all undergraduate women at Northeastern University who are interested in pursuing a career in law! To join, simply register using the form linked below and attend our meetings. Join the slack channel and mailing list to stay updated!
